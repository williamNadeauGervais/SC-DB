Mod version : v1.97

Welcome to SC-Database!

This small local website holds a wealth of information about all the items that you can find during your journey in Shadow Champions. To get started, simply use the menus on the side to see all the items of that category. You can also filter items by rarity.

For more precise information regarding drop rates and locations, refer to the Loot Tables section at the bottom of the menu.

I hope you have fun playing the mod!
- W. N. G.

(2021/02/27) Update Informations :

SC-DB is currently updated to include items from the original Titan Quest game, including its news expansions. Considering many items have been altered or completely reworked, it would only make sense to documented them here. However, it is a long process, and as such SC-DB will be updated bit by bit. Below you'll find more informations as to how the update will be planned.

Step 1 : Helmets - Arms - Torsos - Legs - Rings - Amulets, all MIs and Uniques

Step 2 : Axes - Spears - Bows - Staves - Clubs - Swords - Shields - Offhands - Throwables, all MIs and Uniques

Step 3 : Charms - Relics - Artifacts - Prefixes - Suffixes - Sets

Step 4 : Loot Tables
