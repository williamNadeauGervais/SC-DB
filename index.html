SCDatabase

Mod version : v1.6

Welcome to SC-Database!

This small local website holds a wealth of information about all the items that you can find during your journey in Shadow Champions. To get started, simply use the menus on the side to see all the items of that category. You can also filter items by rarity.

For more precise information regarding drop rates and locations, refer to the Loot Tables section at the bottom of the menu.

I hope you have fun playing the mod!
- W. N. G.
