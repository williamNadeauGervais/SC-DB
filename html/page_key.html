Rogue Dungeon Key ~ Dark Shaman's Secret Hideout

#01

Description:
Use this key to gain access to the Dark Shaman's Secret Hideout dungeon. Once you’re inside, the entrance will remain locked until your session ends - you cannot go back after dying or escaping through a portal. However, the enemies inside have greater chances of yielding rare loot, given that they are also much stronger.

Single use only. The dungeons are only accessible on Epic or Legendary difficulty. You cannot hold multiple instances of this key.

Location: Halcyon Coast
Size: Small
Acquisition: Drops off Fire Magi Satyrs

Related MIs:
  • Fallen Hero's Aegis
  • Fallen Hero's Insignia
  • Lifedrinker's Insignia
  • Pyromancer's Bow
  • Rainmaker's Harness
  • Rainmaker's Mirror
  • Spirit Catcher
Related MUs:
  • Dreadlord’s Cage
  • Mulgor’s Inextinguishable Fury
  • Ogre-Warrior's Armor
  • Sinister Effigy
